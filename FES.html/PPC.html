SEARCH TEAM

Tactical search team trained to locate and rescue individuals in various environments, including urban, wilderness, and disaster settings. Equipped with advanced technology and specialized training, the team is prepared to respond swiftly and effectively to emergencies, ensuring the safety and well-being of those in need.

RECON TEAM

Highly skilled reconnaissance team specializing in gathering critical intelligence and conducting covert operations. Trained in advanced surveillance techniques, the team operates in high-risk environments to provide real-time information on enemy movements, terrain, and potential threats. Equipped with cutting-edge technology, they play a crucial role in strategic planning and mission success.
